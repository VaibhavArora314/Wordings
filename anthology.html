What is an Anthology?

An anthology is a collection of literary works (Poems, Short stories, quotes, open letter, etc.) written by multiple writers or by a single writer. Usually collected in single volume for Publication.

Why get Published in an Anthology?

Not every writer writes a complete Book. That's why you can publish your works in anthologies and be a published author.
